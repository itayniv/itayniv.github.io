About the Project

A Presence is an interactive project that investigates my personal space occupation in my apartment at 1316 EutawPlace, Baltimore MD.

What makes a space a home?
For me, the main ingredient is a work table… Or maybe it's the bed? Perhaps it could be the relationship between the bed and the work table both (the way the clothes travel between each space when the other is used).

In this project I wanted to investigate the way I occupy the apartment space and how I convert it to my own personal space.
I placed a webcam, fixed with a fisheye lens, on various locations of the apartment ceiling, surveilling my movement in the space. The video signal is sent to Processing, translating the difference between the video frames into a graphical language that illustrates the use of the personal space.

The result is then displayed on a graphic, Flash based, user interface.

Enjoy.
